Moroccan Lentil Soup with Mint Yogurt

Price: R 500 per portion

Serving size: 250 grams

Transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Morocco with our Moroccan Lentil Soup. Hearty lentils, simmered with aromatic spices and fragrant herbs, create a soul-warming broth that is both comforting and invigorating. A dollop of refreshing mint yogurt adds a cooling contrast to the warmth of the soup, creating a harmonious balance of flavors. This soup is a celebration of Moroccan cuisine and a delightful start to any meal.
