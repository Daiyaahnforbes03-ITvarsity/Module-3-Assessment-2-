Roasted Red Pepper and Tomato Soup

Price: R 560 per portion

Savor the vibrant flavors of our Roasted Red Pepper and Tomato Soup. Ripe tomatoes and sweet red peppers are roasted to perfection, then pureed into a silky smooth soup that is both comforting and satisfying. A touch of basil and a hint of chili flakes add a subtle kick to the dish, elevating it to new heights of flavor. This soup is a celebration of summer's bounty and a delicious way to start any meal.
